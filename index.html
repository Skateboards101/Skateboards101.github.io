About skateboards

Decks

Maple wood is one of the best wood types for skateboards because it is flexible, yet durable, allowing it to be easily shaped without sacrificing strength.

Trucks

The truck size depends on the size of the deck because if the trucks are longer then the board it can effect flip tricks.

Standard wheels

This type of wheel is most common for skateboards the size can range from 50-75mm

Long board wheels

These wheels are great for long boards, cruisers, and penny boards. these wheels are very smooth on bumps and cracks on the road or sidewalk.

Decks

The best decks are 7-ply which means the board consists of 7 layers of maple wood, the less layers there are the less durable the deck will be.

Trucks

The width of the trucs dependon the width of the deck for example for an 8,25"-8,5" board you need 5,8" (149mm) trucks.
